About Formula 2

FIA Formula 2 (F2) is a single-seater motorsport championship.

  • Cars are similar for all teams (spec series).
  • Races support Formula 1 weekends.
  • Drivers are usually young and trying to reach F1.

How it works

Race weekends include practice, qualifying, and races.

  1. Practice: Testing and preparation.
  2. Qualifying: Determines the grid.
  3. Sprint & Feature races: Two-race format.

Basic Rules

  • All teams use similar chassis and engines.
  • Points decide the season champion.
  • Pit stops matter for strategy.
  • Drivers must follow all safety rules.
